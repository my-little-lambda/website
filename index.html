  • Currying
  • Types
  • Languages that are Functional by default

About the Author

Why I am writing this book

I have successfully taught Functional Programming to several teams. Over the years I have given talks, slides, made projects to help people learn this material. I have decided to put that in a book.

Most people get hung up on the new terms and concepts they hear, making it painful to learn. Once you learn these concepts, you will understand why a lot of people enjoy Functional Programming.

A little bit about me

I am currently a Senior Software Engineer at PlayStation. After joining PlayStation I became a technical lead, and currently lead and mentor a team of 6 engineers.
